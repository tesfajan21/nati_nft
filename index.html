ROAD MAP

People cannot just be tied together, they have to connect and that is our collection all about: CONNECTING PEOPLE AND HAVING A PEERLESS COMMUNITY. Our main priority is the community, to have a healthy and compassionate one. We want to create a community that will also give voice to the voiceless and make a positive impact to the society.

Phase One

Building Community and Project Establishment

  • Establish team and strategise marketing plan to ensure the success of the project. Building a healthy and compassionate community.
  • Launch Mursi Girl website, Discord and social media profiles

Phase Two

10% Minted

  • Improve and boost DISCORD to have a better place for the community to connect. To have more engagement with our members and have a positive influence in their lives.

20% Minted

  • Create more promotional content and boost the collection in all social media platform. Show the world that our collection is a LIFESTYLE.

70% Minted

  • Donate $300 each to 5 charitable organisations.
  • Host contest exclusively for members. (Prize: ETH, exclusive NFTs)

Phase Three

MEMBER PERKS AND BENEFITS

  • Host major contests for the members (fan art, meme, interactive contests and many more) (Prize: ETH, Vacation Trip, special release NFTs, Physical Gifts, etc)
  • Release of Rarity Ranking Table
  • Special releases, add-ons and upgrades in a form of airdrops.

Phase Four

PROJECT EXPANSION

  • Create a TEAM and expand our project to further build our community and continuously create potential and infinite opportunities for everyone.
  • Partner with brands and public figures to create exclusive opportunities for our members.
